Who We Are

Beneath the Impact is a storytelling and learning platform dedicated to empowering changemakers. We believe in the power of narratives to drive meaningful change and foster a deeper understanding of the world around us. Our platform serves as a hub for sharing impactful stories, facilitating learning experiences, and connecting individuals committed to making a difference.

Our Mission

Our mission is to amplify the voices of those working on the front lines of change, providing a platform for their stories to be heard and shared. We aim to create a space where individuals can learn from diverse perspectives, gain insights into complex issues, and connect with others who share a passion for creating a better future.

Demonstrate Your Organization's Impact

Build Learning Systems for Ongoing Change. Organizations often struggle to show the impact of their work. This fellowship, co-designed with organizations at every growth stage, provides practical tools to integrate data collection and learning into your activities.

Why This Fellowship

Many organizations find it challenging to collect data and demonstrate the changes their programs are creating. This can lead to unclear reporting and missed opportunities for improvement.

The Impact Measurement Compass Fellowship addresses this by helping you establish learning systems and cultures integrated into your daily activities. Co-designed with organizations like yours, it focuses on data collection to reveal ongoing impact, support informed decisions, and foster a space for reflection and adaptation.

Modules Overview

Rooted in approaches like model thinking, human-centered design, and systems thinking, this fellowship guides you through a practical pathway to establish learning systems and cultures.

target

Center: Data and Culture as the Core

Support a practical mindset in your team: Make data collection feel accessible and useful, not overwhelming. Set up learning processes that connect your goals to everyday decisions, allowing your organization to reflect and adjust as needed, regardless of your starting point.

explore

North: Purpose

A. Understanding the Problem, Why We Measure Impact

Clarify your mission: Use tools like personas and journey maps to identify community needs, define key issues, and establish a focus that aligns with stakeholders. Document existing efforts into clear narratives that support ongoing work.

analytics

East: Evidence

B. Linking Projects to the Problem, What We Measure & How

Streamline data processes: Develop theories of change and indicators linked to your activities, integrating data collection with available tools. Address stakeholder priorities to gather evidence that reflects real progress.

trending_up

South: Action

C. Embracing Experimentation, Applying Data for Adaptation

Incorporate experimentation: Try small pilots, review outcomes thoughtfully, and make adjustments based on findings. Address common hesitations to support incremental improvements, tailored for organizations handling detailed reporting.

share

West: Learning

D. Sharing Learning for Influence, Sensemaking, Validation & Influence

Share findings effectively: Conduct sessions to validate with communities, then create materials like infographics or briefs to present data. Monitor responses to refine your approach, helpful for teams contributing to sector discussions.

Who It's For

Designed for teams ready to integrate learning systems, this fellowship meets you at your current stage.

Stage 1: Early Ideation

Passion drives the work, but evidence is limited. We provide basic tools to document initial activities.

Stage 2: Growing Operations

Accountability grows, but systems are still informal. Focus on straightforward data collection to show progress without added strain.

Stage 3: Scaling Complexity

Requirements for monitoring, evaluation, and reporting become more complex. Get adapted frameworks to demonstrate ongoing impact.

Stage 4: Leadership Influence

Organizations shape agendas and show measurable effects. Support sharing evidence with peers and partners.

Teams of 2+ (leader + practitioner) from NGOs or social enterprises committed to ethical, community-centered impact. Hungry for 4-6 months of transformative learning (1-2 hours weekly on self-paced content, plus monthly coaching) to turn insights into action.
